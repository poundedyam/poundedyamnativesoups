Special Dish Menu

Each dish promises an unforgettable dining experience, blending innovation with tradition to delight your senses.

SeaFood Egusi Soup

My Lumpy Egusi is a classic soup from all around Nigeria prepared with bumptious melon seeds steamed in the most succulent meat options and disturbances in a pool of heat-caressed palm oil. It is the perfect partner to many swallow meals.

Afia Efere (with Chicken)

Afia Efere (with Chicken) is a traditional soup from the Efik people of Southern Nigeria prepared from roasted Uyayak pods in order to get maximum flavor. You probably also know it as Ofe Nsala, the Igbo name for it. It is the perfect partner to many swallow meals.

Assorted Meat Peppersoup

Our Assorted Meat Pepper soup is a party favorite loved all around Nigeria prepared from the finest meat innards and disturbances in a pool of sweet peppers. It is perfect on its own or as a partner to many rice meals
